Mission Requirements:

At Mission: MARS, our mission is to bring people to our closest neighbor in the solar system, and to begin a colony on Mars.
Our main focus is the trip and preparation of our astronauts for the trip, as well as the training required beforehand.
Below are some requirements for being a part of the crew, or to be a passenger.

To be a shuttle Pilot:

  • Must be over 25
  • Must weigh under 220 lbs
  • Must not have any preexisting conditions (Athsma, heart conditions, etc.)
  • Must have and present proof of prior flight experience (Military, professional, etc.)
  • Must pass astronaut piloting simulator exam

To Qualify as a passenger:

  • Must be over 18
  • Must weigh under 220 lbs
  • Must not have any preexisting conditions (Athsma, heart conditions, etc.)
  • Must pass multiple medical tests for any viruses/diseases; these have the potential to be passed to the crew or other passengers

Required Subjects:

Passengers and Crew will be required to test well in the following skills in order to participate as well:

  1. Physics
  2. Advanced Mathematics
  3. Intense Problem Solving/Critical Thinking
